RSVP App

The idea here is to give a simple multiple choice question for how likely you are to attend a thing. Each option should be understandable to normal people but also include a range of probabilities. For example: 

(a) I'm definitely coming! (>90% chance)
(b) I'm probably coming (>50% chance)
(c) I'm unlikely this time but want to stay in the loop (<50% chance)
